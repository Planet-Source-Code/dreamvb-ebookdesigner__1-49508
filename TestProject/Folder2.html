Renaming a Folder in Windows.

Ok now following on from our last exercise of creating our folder. I will now explain how we can rename and delete a folder.

To rename a folder click on your new created folder right click on the folder you will then see a menu form this menu select rename. You will then see that the folder text is then highlighted as shown in this screen show below:


While the text is still highlighted type in any name you wont e.g. "Test Folder" without the quotes. For the new folder name to take effect you will need to press the enter key. There you go you have just renamed a folder.

We now move on to deleting a folder. To do this is simple simply right click on your folder and select Delete from the menu. You will now see a dialog box appear on the screen asking, "if your sure you want to delete the folder" as shown in the screen shot below:



For this example we can simplay click on the Yes button. That's it your folder has now been deleted. anway I hoped you liked this little tutor.

O just one quick note before I go if you ever deleted the folder by mistake just simply right click your mouse and select Undo Delete, This will then bring back your folder unless you have emptyed your recycle bin.
